Veggie Facts
  • Every single dairy product comes from milk!
  • Dairy products are excelent sources of calcium, protein, potassium, and vitamin D
  • Cows provide more than 90% of the world's milk supply. Each U.S. dairy cow provides 2,000 gallons of milk per year
  • 77% of girls and 64% of boys aged 12 to 15 years old are not consuming the recommended daily requirement for calcium
  • A fruit is the product of a flowering plant and contain the seeds of that plant. This is what makes a fruit a fruit!
  • Examples of dairy include milk, yogurt, cheese, and even ice cream
