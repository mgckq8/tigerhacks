Fruit Facts
  • Tomatoes are actually fruits!!!
  • Applesauce was the very first food eaten by an American astronaut in space
  • Beans, nuts, and legumes are included in a subcategory of fruits properly named as dry fruits
  • A fruit is the product of a flowering plant and contain the seeds of that plant. This is what makes a fruit a fruit!
