Fat and Sugar Facts
  • Fats are incredibly important components to a healthy diet. In fact, it shoud account for 30% of it
  • There are three kinds of fats. Unsaturated fats are found in plants and fish. Saturated fats are found in animal products. Trans fats are found in margarine and are often major compontents of snack foods.
  • The fat within you body is what fuels you. It stores the energy necessary for day to day activities
  • Fat can be expelled by breathing it out as well as burning it off when we lose weight
