Grains

Grain Facts
  • There are seven major types of grains; corn, wheat, rice, barley, sorghum, oats, and rye
  • Corn is the most produced grain, followed by wheat and then rice. Not only is corn an ingredient to many products, it is also food for much of our livestock
  • Wheat is grown on all continents excluding Antarctica
  • The first pregnancy tests recorded in Ancient Egypt using wheat.
  • The Great Wall of China was built using sticky rice as a sort of mortar.
